About

Independent advice before you commit.

I am Edvard Pregun, Managing Director of 2100 Projects Pty Ltd. I work with CIOs, CTOs and IT Directors in mid-market Australian organisations.

Over 30 years I have delivered ICT programs across Defence, Banking, Government, Energy, Aviation and Telecommunications. I have seen what works, what fails, and what questions get skipped when a vendor is in the room.

2100 Projects exists to ask those questions before anyone commits. No vendor partnerships. No preferred integrators. No referral arrangements. Every recommendation is based solely on what is right for your organisation.

The best technology investment is one that grows what your organisation can do, not just reduces what it costs to run. My goal is not to make your organisation smaller. It is to help you get more capability, more value and more return from the technology decisions you make.
